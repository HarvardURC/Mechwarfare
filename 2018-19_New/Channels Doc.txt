Channel of radio controller - Pin on Teensy - Semantic meaning

CH1 - pin 23 - JAM_CHANNEL, and tilt (continuous control)
CH2 - pin 22 - pan (continuous control)
CH3 - pin 21 - vx
CH4 - pin 19 - yaw
CH5 - pin 18 - GUN_CHANNEL
CH6 - pin 17 - vy
CH7 - pin 16 - not used
CH8 - pin 15 - IDLE_SWITCH

To see how the channels of radio controller map to buttons/sticks on the controller, look into the controller menu.
